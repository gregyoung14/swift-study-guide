# Project Management (Agile, Scrum)

## Overview
This section provides a comprehensive guide to project management methodologies, with a focus on Agile and Scrum, essential for senior iOS engineers to effectively lead and contribute to development teams. We will explore the principles, practices, and roles within these frameworks, enabling you to optimize workflows, foster collaboration, and deliver high-quality software efficiently.
